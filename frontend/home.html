Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FitnessCRM

Do I need a credit card for the free trial?

No! Start your 14-day free trial without entering any payment information. We'll only ask for payment details if you decide to continue after the trial.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate the billing accordingly.

Is my client data secure?

Yes! We use bank-level encryption and security measures to protect your data. All information is stored on secure servers with regular backups.

Do you offer customer support?

Yes! All plans include email support. Professional and Enterprise plans get priority support and phone access. We're here to help you succeed.

Can I import my existing client data?

Yes! We provide easy data import tools and can help you migrate from other platforms. Contact our support team for assistance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover) and support international payments in multiple currencies.
